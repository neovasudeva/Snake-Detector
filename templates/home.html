Welcome!

Welcome! snake.ai is a simple machine learning model built with Detectron2 used for real time object detection of snakes. You can upload a photo of a snake to see if the model can detect it. Do not upload anything that you don't want the developer to see! While images you upload are not stored anywhere, future updates plan on saving some uploaded images to further train the model. For current issues with the model and future updates, visit the About page.

To start, upload an image and wait a couple of seconds while the model tries to find snakes in the image. Currently, the model can identify snakes given images of one, but performs terribly when given images without snakes. This will be resolved in later updates.
